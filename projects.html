Projects

We choose problems where careful engineering changes outcomes in the real world.

Project 001 — SafeSpeed BlackBox

Problem: Overspeeding and fatigue create avoidable incidents while evidence remains disputed.

Approach: A compact black-box that records secure event summaries and trusted signals — enough to learn and prove, without exposing people.

  • Benefit Fewer incidents; fairer outcomes when they occur.
  • Buyer Fleets and insurers who pay for reduced loss ratio.
  • Design Edge evidence, encrypted by default.

Project 002 — ArchiPrint

Problem: Architecture teams lose days between idea, model, and feedback.

Approach: A practical pipeline from sketch/CAD to optimized prints — compressing review cycles and material waste.

  • Who Architects, developers, planners.
  • Value Faster iteration with tangible models.
  • Model Tools + materials margin.

Project 003 — NeuroWake

Problem: Drowsiness builds quietly and then suddenly matters.

Approach: A gentle ear-worn device that recognizes pre-sleep patterns and helps drivers stay alert — private by design.

  • Fit Logistics, taxis, and professional drivers.
  • Compliance Built for CE marking.
  • Moat Careful signal processing with adaptive response.
